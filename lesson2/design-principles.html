White Space and Clean Design

Fry's Food Stores

Frysfood.com

Fry's website uses white space to bring focus to the icons, subheadings and food items displayed on the page. The white space creates a contrast for the viewer to focus on the point of the site, which is to lookup items/ads and shop the store online.
