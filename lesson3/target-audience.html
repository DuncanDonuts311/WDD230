Target Audience

The target audience for this website is anyone who has internet access and lives in or around DeWitt, Virginia. To be more specific, the ideal audience, is those in DeWitt, Virginia that own, operate, or want to support the small businesses in the area

Persona 1:

Persona:

The Newly Moved Couple

Fictional Name:

Jack Jefferson

Job Title:

Licensed Contractor

Demographics:

  • 40 years old
  • 2 children with a third on the way
  • Self employed contractor that chose to raise his children in a more rural environment

Goals and Tasks:

Wants to start networking with his neighbors to support their business and to find some business of his own

  • Support his neighbors businesses
  • Find jobs in his local area

Environment:

Jack is use to being surrounded by neighborhoods and big businesses where someone is always looking to complete a home improvement project or a remodel in their home. He did not realize how isolated he would be on a 10 acre property without neighbors he could easily approach and get to know

Scenarios:

  • Jack has been driving close to an hour each way to find clients that he is familiar with so he can continue to make money and support his family. His new neighbors are so far away that he does not feel comfortable approaching them or knocking on their door. He is tired of all the driving and not getting to spend enough time with his family. He wants to find jobs closer to home and is looking to network with his fellow small business owners in the area. He is looking for resources to meet his neighbors and find work closer to his new home.

Persona 2:

Persona:

Grandma

Fictional Name:

Jean Grey

Job Title:

Retired | Avid Gardener

Demographics:

  • 66 years old
  • Widowed
  • 5 children

Goals and Tasks:

She loves to grow all types of plants and has recently been gardening for a lot of her own produce.
She enjoys:

  • Hosting family dinners and making delicious food from plants she grew herself
  • Chatting with neighbors when she sees them around town

Environment:

Jean has been widowed for almost a decade and lives in a house by herself in a very remote area. She has always had a green thumb and uses a lot of her property for gardening. She does not get to interact with a lot of people on a regular basis, unless her children are visiting

Scenarios:

  • Jean has been growing a lot of her own food for a long time and loves to garden. It is one of the few things that keep her active but she has been wasting a lot of her home grown produce recently because she is cultivating more than she can eat by herself. She has been thinking about selling some of her produce but isn't sure where to start. She is retired and isn't looking for a full time job but could use a little bit more money each month. She wonders if any of her neighbors would be interested in buying her extra produce and is looking for a way to get to know them and interact with them on a more regular basis
